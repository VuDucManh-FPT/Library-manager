Introduction to the FPT University Library in Hanoi

The library was officially established under Decision No. 1029/QD-DHFPT dated December 9, 2015. The functions and tasks of the library are as follows:

Functions:

The library operates as an integrated learning resource center built to effectively support teaching, research and academic information activities for FPT staff, lecturers and students.

ư

Mission:

Collect, supplement, process, inform and provide documents, scientific information in many fields to effectively support teaching, research and academic activities.

Ensure users receive accurate and timely information to meet their needs, including conducting user needs assessments for lecturers, researchers, postgraduates and undergraduates.

Consult and plan short-term and long-term strategies for information services in the organization.

Organize, store and preserve library collections, including all types of publications and media resources.

Develop appropriate information retrieval systems to effectively serve and disseminate information to users.

Collect and archive university-produced publications, including theses and dissertations.

Promote partnerships and direct collaboration with national and international information centers, scientific organizations and universities.

  • Library Collection:

    Access to 4 databases (DB):

    Catalog Database (OPAC): Search for textbooks and reference books available in the library (physical books). Instructions are available at this link.

    Internal Database (Dspace): Access to students' graduation projects, course materials, scientific articles, etc. Instructions are available at this link.

    Online Books Database (Books24x7): This database is purchased annually by FPT University, including topics such as IT, engineering, economics, finance, etc. Instructions are available at this link.
